Command: raidReserves(raidName, discordName(officersOnly, optional))

1. raidName = Name of the raid for which you'd like to be in reserves.
2. discordName = If you're trying to put another person in reserves. (officersOnly, optional)

Add author of message/discordName to the raidSignup as a reserve.

Example:
'~raidReserves trainingRaid'
