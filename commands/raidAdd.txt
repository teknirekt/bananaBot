Command: raidAdd(raidName, roleIndex, roleDescription, discordName(officersOnly, optional))

1. raidName = Name of the raid for which you're trying to sign up for.
2. roleIndex = The spot # you wish to sign up for.
3. roleDescription = What you're bringing to said spot.
4. discordName = If you're trying to sign up another person. (officersOnly, optional)

Add author of message/discordName to the specified spot at raidSignup.
Intended for **single spot** signups. If you want intervals, check out raidFill.

Example:
'~raidAdd trainingRaid 7 CondiSoulbeast'
