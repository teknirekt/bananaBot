raidAdd(raidName, raidSpot, role)

newRaid(raidName)
deleteRaid(raidName)
raidSetup(raidName, day, date, time, timezone)

Note: 
1. All commands should be executed using the prefix. 
2. All commands are case-sensitive.
3. All arguments are seperated by spaces.
